SCENE II
Tartuffe, Dorine
Tartuffe

[observing Dorine].

lock up my hair shirt and my scourge, and pray for freedom from each carnal urge.If anyone comes calling, say I have gone to share my alms with the poor souls in prison.

Dorine

[aside].

affectation and boastful behavior!

Tartuffe.

What do you wish?

Dorine.

To say . . .

Tartuffe

[taking a handkerchief from his pocket].

By our Savior, Please! Before you speak take this handkerchief.
